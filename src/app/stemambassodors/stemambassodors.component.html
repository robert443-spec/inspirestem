WHO ARE STEM AMBASSODORS?
The STEM ambassadors are volunteers from wide range of Science,Technology,Engineering and Mathematics 
fields In Tanzania. They offer their time to help bring STEM to life and demonstrates its important.
 STEM ambassadors mentor, train and offer expertise to young people so as To inspire them in STEM careers.
University students and graduates in STEM fields, STEM Teachers, STEM experts and employers in STEM Companies can join as STEM ambassadors
STEM Ambassadors Impact on young people
Young people participation in STEM.
Young people awareness of STEM and its careers.
Young people innovations.
